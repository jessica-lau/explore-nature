Our Mission: We want you to unplug and reconnect with nature.

Explore Nature wants every individual to reconnect with the outdoors. We know it's important to step back from reality and refresh your mind every once in a while. That's why we created this program to set people out on an adventure for 15 days. No internet, no cell phone. Just nature. Individuals will join activities such as rock climbing, ziplining, and rafting. Come join us for a life-changing experience and meet lifelong friends.
