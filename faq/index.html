Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I sign up to join an Explore Nature adventure?

Please give us a call and we will get you started. Sign up is per request and application forms will be sent through your email once necessary information is verified.

How much does it costs to join an Explore Nature adventure?

Pricing starts at $3,000 - all inclusive. The cost varies on the location that we are offering at the time when application completed.

I have my dog that I would like to bring with me. Is that allowed?

Our team at Explore Nature loves companions. If you have a pet that is well trained and would like to bring with you, you are more than welcome! Please note that you are responsible for providing all your pet's needs and food.

Are we allowed to have cell phones during our stay?

Explore Nature's goal is to disconnect you from technology and reconnect you with nature. We advise that you please refrain from bringing any technology with you. In the case of emergency that you need to contact someone, we provide devices for use at our offices.
